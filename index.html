My research mainly focuses on subsurface scattering and light transport in Participating medium. Also I am interested in offline/ real-time global illumination.

Education and Experiences

  • Associate professor, Nanjing University of Science and Technology (March. 2017).
  • Postdoc, under the supervision of Nicolas Holzschuch, INRIA, France (Nov. 2015 - Feb. 2017).
  • Working on game technique research and development( Disney Infinity 3), Studio Gobo, UK (Dec. 2014 - October. 2015).
  • Visiting Ph.D. student, under the supervision of Tamy Boubekeur, CG Group, Télécom ParisTech, France (Oct. 2012 - Oct. 2014).
  • Intership in OGS Team, Autodesk, Shanhai, China (Nov. 2010 - May. 2011).
  • Ph.D., under the supervison of Xiangxu Meng, comptuer science and technology, Shandong University, China (Dec. 2014).
  • Bachelor degree, software engineering, Shandong University, China ( Jun. 2009).
